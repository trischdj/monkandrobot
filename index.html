Robot

Monk + Robot is an exploratory space where conviviality and computation merge in collaborations between D.J. Trischler and AI. The relationship is inspired by the delightfully optimistic bond between Dex (a tea monk seeking wild spaces) and Splendid Speckled Mosscap (a robot interested in humanity) in Becky Chambers' Psalm for the Wild Built, as well as by Ivan Illich's Tools for Conviviality.

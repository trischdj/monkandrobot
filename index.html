Monk & Robot

Monk & Robot is an exploratory space where contemplation and computation merge in Tools for Conviviality (See Ivan Illich), designed and built by D.J. Trischler and AI (and often other bipedal mammals). The collaboration is inspired by the delightfully optimistic relationship between Dex (a monk) and Splendid Speckled Mosscap (a robot) in the solar punk, post-transition, future vividly articulated by Becky Chambers in Psalm for the Wild Built books.
